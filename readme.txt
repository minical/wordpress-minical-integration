Minical is an opensource Property Management System (PMS) and Online Booking System for hotels.

== Description ==      

Minical Hotel Booking plugin is a fully customizable booking form with online payments, notifications, and Calendar sync. It allows you to place real-time availability calendars, property management, a directory of accommodations, and the booking engine on your website to drive direct rental property bookings.
The Minical Hotel Booking plugin is completely optimized to suit the needs of different short-term rental types. With this plugin, you can rent out unlimited vacation rentals or hotel rooms, accommodations or beds, pet boarding rooms or campsites, beds, breakfasts, guest houses. The plugin makes it a breeze to automate bookings, schedule arrivals, and get paid in advance by your guests.
This WordPress booking plugin comes with all the needed functionality to run a fully functional hospitality business website, enable payments and manage reservations in a centralized dashboard.
So if you’re ready to automate booking on your (or your client’s) website, MiniCal could be the appointment software you’re looking for. your clients can make bookings for service right on your site. No need for time-consuming phone calls or face-to-face meetings.

**The default Features of Minical Hotel Booking includes:**

*   Create a beautiful listing of all your properties.
*   Add unlimited accommodations, accepts unlimited bookings without you or your guest paying any extra commissions.
*   Control room types and rates based on different parameters.
*   Synchronize direct site reservations with popular travel channels (e.g., Channex, Airbnb, or Booking.com,) with the admin channel manager.
*   Customize the property and display availability calendars.
*   Allow guests to book properties and schedule arrivals online with or without instant payments via multiple integrated payment gateways.
*   Add numerous booking rules, e.g., the time for arrival/departure, the min/max days to stay and more; take properties out of booking when needed.
*   View business performance stats in the sales report dashboards.
*   Send out automated branded emails.
*   Download bookings data in CSV.

== Installation ==

* First of all download the plugin ZIP file.
* Log in to your WordPress admin panel.
* Go to your Plugins page, then click Add New > Upload Plugin.
* Click "Choose file" then select the plugin zip file.
* After that Install and activate our plugin.
